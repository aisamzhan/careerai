## Industries

Kazakhstan has a mixed economy where natural resources, services, and emerging technology sectors all play important roles.

### Industry: Energy, Oil and Gas
- Overview (KZ context): One of the most important sectors; Kazakhstan is a major producer of oil, gas, uranium, and other natural resources. Contributes a large share of GDP and exports.
- Typical professions: petroleum engineers, drilling engineers, mechanical engineers, project managers, geologists, safety engineers.
- Where jobs are concentrated (cities/regions): western regions such as Atyrau, Aktau, Aktobe.
- Salary notes: typically among the highest in the country.
- Employment notes: employment volume is smaller than service sectors.

### Industry: Information Technology (IT)
- Overview (KZ context): rapidly growing due to digitalization and government support for the technology sector.
- Major activities: software development, web development, mobile application development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud computing.
- Where jobs are concentrated (cities/regions): Almaty and Astana have the largest IT job markets.
- Demand signals: demand for skilled developers continues to grow.

### Industry: Finance and Banking
- Overview (KZ context): developed financial sector; Almaty is considered the financial center of the country.
- Typical roles: accountants, financial analysts, auditors, risk managers, investment analysts.
- Notes: roles require strong analytical skills and knowledge of financial systems.

### Industry: Trade and Retail
- Overview (KZ context): employs a very large portion of the workforce.
- Typical jobs: sales assistants, retail managers, customer service specialists, e-commerce managers.
- Entry roles: retail jobs are often entry-level positions for students and young professionals.

### Industry: Healthcare
- Overview (KZ context): major public sector employer.
- Typical roles: doctors, nurses, pharmacists, medical technicians, hospital administrators.
- Demand signals: demand is constant due to population needs.

### Industry: Education
- Overview (KZ context): one of the largest sectors in terms of employment.
- Typical professions: school teachers, university lecturers, tutors, education administrators.
- Demand notes: teachers are frequently needed in both urban and rural regions.

### Industry: Transport and Logistics
- Overview (KZ context): Kazakhstan is a major transit country connecting Europe and Asia, creating demand for logistics and transportation professionals.
- Typical roles: truck drivers, logistics managers, supply chain analysts, warehouse managers.

### Industry: Construction and Infrastructure
- Overview (KZ context): infrastructure development drives demand for construction professionals.
- Common roles: civil engineers, construction managers, architects, project supervisors.

### Industry: Agriculture
- Overview (KZ context): remains important especially in rural areas.
- Typical roles: agronomists, farm managers, agricultural engineers, livestock specialists.
