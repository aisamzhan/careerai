# Kazakhstan Labour Market: Salary Ranges & Career Paths

Last updated: 2026-03-15
Sources: User-provided text ("Kazakhstan Labour Market Knowledge Base (for CareerAI AI System)")

## Salary Ranges

Salary levels vary depending on industry, experience, and city. Major cities such as Almaty and Astana generally offer higher salaries.

### Typical salary levels (all industries, per month)
- Entry level (0–2 years experience): 150k – 400k KZT
- Mid level (3–7 years experience): 400k – 900k KZT
- Senior level (8+ years experience): 900k – 2M+ KZT

Note: Certain industries such as oil and gas may offer higher salaries.

### Profession: Software Developer
- Demand level: High
- Entry level: 250k – 450k KZT
- Mid level: 500k – 900k KZT
- Senior level: 1M – 2M+ KZT

### Profession: Data Analyst
- Demand level: Growing
- Entry level: 250k – 400k KZT
- Mid level: 400k – 700k KZT
- Senior level: 700k – 1.2M KZT

### Profession: Accountant
- Demand level: High
- Entry level: 180k – 300k KZT
- Mid level: 350k – 600k KZT
- Senior level: 700k – 1M+ KZT

### Profession: Mechanical Engineer
- Demand level: Stable
- Entry level: 250k – 400k KZT
- Mid level: 450k – 800k KZT
- Senior level: 900k – 1.5M KZT

### Profession: Teacher
- Demand level: High
- Entry level: 140k – 200k KZT
- Mid level: 200k – 350k KZT
- Senior level: 350k – 500k KZT

### Profession: Logistics Manager
- Demand level: Growing
- Entry level: 200k – 350k KZT
- Mid level: 350k – 600k KZT
- Senior level: 700k – 1.2M KZT

### Profession: Backend Developer (Node / Java / .NET)
- Junior: 200k – 300k KZT
- Middle: 500k – 650k KZT
- Senior: 1M – 2M+ KZT
- Notes: High competition (especially at junior level)
- Market reality: high demand in Almaty/Astana; junior market can be oversaturated

### Profession: Frontend Developer (React)
- Junior: 250k – 350k KZT
- Middle: 400k – 700k KZT
- Senior: 1M – 2M+ KZT
- Market reality: high competition for juniors; portfolio is critical

### Profession: DevOps Engineer
- Junior: 300k – 500k KZT
- Middle: 700k – 1.2M KZT
- Senior: 1.5M+ KZT
- Market reality: lower competition than dev roles; high demand

### Profession: Product Manager
- Junior: 400k – 600k KZT
- Middle: 700k – 1.2M KZT
- Senior: 1.5M+ KZT
- Market reality: often requires prior experience (not purely entry-level)

### Profession: SMM Manager
- Junior: 150k – 300k KZT
- Middle: 300k – 600k KZT
- Senior: 700k+ KZT
- Market reality: high competition; portfolio can matter more than a diploma

## Career Paths

### Software Development Career Path
- Junior Developer → Middle Developer → Senior Developer → Tech Lead → Engineering Manager
- Notes: Some developers transition into product management or startup leadership.

### Data Analysis Career Path
- Junior Data Analyst → Data Analyst → Senior Data Analyst → Data Scientist → Head of Data / Chief Data Officer

### Business and Management Career Path
- Business Assistant → Specialist (HR / Marketing / Finance) → Senior Specialist / Team Lead → Department Manager → Director

### Engineering Career Path
- Junior Engineer → Engineer → Senior Engineer → Project Manager → Engineering Director
